## Hi, I'm Mateo

I'm a designer. Usually that means asking uncomfortable questions until something gets simpler.

Most of my job is noticing what everyone agreed not to mention.

What if cursors told time? What if keeping a portfolio updated wasn't so hard? What if I stopped waiting for perfect and just shipped things?

*Twenty years in. Between roles, open to what's next.*

## I build things on the side

To stay curious, and because the alternative is rust. No startups, no audiences, no roadmap. Some are useful. Some are just weird. All of them taught me something.
