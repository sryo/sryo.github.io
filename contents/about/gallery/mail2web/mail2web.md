Emails have always been an **intuitive self-publishing tool**, yet confined to specific recipients.

To overcome this, I wrote a [Google Apps script](https://github.com/sryo/GmailScripts/blob/master/email2Web.gs) that **adds a public folder to your Gmail account**. This endeavor highlights the innovation possible when we **reimagine the potential of everyday tools**.
