date: 2023-05-22
tags: [AI, Google Apps Script, Email]
---

For people drowning in mail. [GmailTidy](https://github.com/sryo/GmailTidy) watches what you do, then starts doing the same.

**Hot**. Your inbox is for what's actually important.

**Ping**. Resurfaces threads you forgot to answer.

**Bunch**. A label per domain appears as you receive mail. For the search that ends in "from:@company.com".

**Stash**. For the attachment you can't find. Threads with files in their own shelf.

**Riff**. For the email you've already written in your head. A draft is waiting, learned from how you write.
