---
title: "Coro: What if the terminal had a top-down view?"
date: 2026-05-22
tags: [TypeScript, Claude Code, Agents, Worktrees]
---

A terminal shows one task at a time. Run five agents and that's five windows to keep track of.

[Coro](https://github.com/sryo/coro) lays them out instead. Each card is a Claude Code conversation in its own worktree, and you see all of them at once. Auto-commit and dependency chains let cards keep working without you.
