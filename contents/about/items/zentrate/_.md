People spend 10+ hours a day on screens, hooked by notifications, feeds, and apps competing for attention.

[ZenTrate](https://github.com/sryo/scriptables) is a set of iOS widgets that replaces cluttered icon grids with a **calm verb-based palette**: "Pay" instead of opening a bank app, "Set Timer" instead of the clock. Built with Scriptable, inspired by **digital minimalism** and old point-and-click adventure games.
