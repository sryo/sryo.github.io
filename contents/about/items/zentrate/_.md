I started replacing my iOS home screen icons with verbs. "Pay" instead of opening a bank app. "Set Timer" instead of the clock.

[ZenTrate](https://github.com/sryo/scriptables) is the set of Scriptable widgets that came out of it, inspired by old point-and-click adventure games.
