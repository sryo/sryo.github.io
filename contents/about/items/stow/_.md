title: "Stow: What if you could bring your own tab bar?"
date: 2026-02-24
tags: [Swift, macOS, Browsers]
---

Browsers want to own your tabs. Switch from Chrome to Safari and your "important reading" pile becomes inaccessible.

Stow is a workspace-based bookmark manager. Tabs live in workspaces you carry across browsers and machines. Open a workspace and the right set of tabs lands wherever you are. The browser hosts them; it doesn't own them.
