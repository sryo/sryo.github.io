---
title: "Dirpad: What if you could swipe to navigate the terminal?"
date: 2026-02-07
tags: [Swift, macOS, Terminal, Input]
---

fzf, autocomplete, file pickers, vim. Every fast-CLI workflow eventually becomes "press the arrow key 47 times." Your trackpad is sitting there with way more bandwidth than four keys.

[Dirpad](https://github.com/sryo/dirpad) maps trackpad gestures to arrow keys at the OS level. Swipe down a list of matches in fzf at the speed of a flick. The trackpad already knows how to be fast. Dirpad just lets it talk to apps in a language they already speak.
