Two Figma plugins from one hack day, both fixing things that bugged me about other plugins.

[Anchorify](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1284214046492867185/anchorify) converts `&#91;text&#93;(url)` and `[url=url]text[/url]` markup into clickable hyperlinks. Google Sheets Sync was the closest thing I'd found and it stops short of doing this.

Another plugin, [Word Hugger](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1285425464785405153/word-hugger), **fixes orphaned words** (those lonely single words on a line) by grouping together the last couple of words. Because every word deserves company.
