---
title: "Spoons: What if every macOS annoyance had a fix?"
date: 2023-05-25
tags: [Lua, Hammerspoon, macOS]
---

I keep a folder of small things macOS does wrong, and another of the Hammerspoon scripts I write to fix them. The second folder is at [github.com/sryo/Spoons](https://github.com/sryo/Spoons).

**[FrameMaster](https://github.com/sryo/Spoons/blob/main/FrameMaster.lua)**. For the people who agree that the four most reachable spots on the screen shouldn't sit empty.

**[WindowScape](https://github.com/sryo/Spoons/tree/main/WindowScape)**. For people who want their windows to take care of themselves. Open one, the others shrink; close it, the rest fill in.

**[WanderFocus](https://github.com/sryo/Spoons/blob/main/WanderFocus.lua)**. For people who think hovering a window should be enough to activate it.

**[BubbleCursor](https://github.com/sryo/Spoons/blob/main/BubbleCursor.lua)**. For when the target is small and your aim isn't. The cursor finds the nearest interactive thing and forwards clicks.

**[EdgeHopper](https://github.com/sryo/Spoons/blob/main/EdgeHopper.lua)**. I built it for the wide displays where dragging the cursor across feels like a chore. Push against any edge and it warps to the opposite side.

**[SideSwipe](https://github.com/sryo/Spoons/blob/main/SideSwipe.lua)**. Puts the brightness and volume controls on the trackpad edges, for the version of you that finds the function row a small inconvenience. 

**[Sssssssscroll](https://github.com/sryo/Spoons/blob/main/Sssssssscroll.lua)**. For the times you need to scroll and your hands aren't free.

**[Muse](https://github.com/sryo/Spoons/tree/main/Muse)**. For the people who switch to ChatGPT a hundred times a day. Adds AI to any input field.

**[DigUp](https://github.com/sryo/Spoons/tree/main/DigUp)**. For when you remember reading something but not where. DigUp keeps a local, searchable log of every pixel you've seen.

**[CloudPad](https://github.com/sryo/Spoons/tree/main/CloudPad)**. For the times your phone is closer than your keyboard. CloudPad turns it into one.

**[HyperlinkHijacker](https://github.com/sryo/Spoons/blob/main/HyperlinkHijacker.lua)**. For the times your default browser isn't the right one.

**[UndoClose](https://github.com/sryo/Spoons/blob/main/UndoClose.lua)**. For the moments right after Cmd-Q when you wish you hadn't. UndoClose brings the app back if you act within five seconds.

**[AutoDMG](https://github.com/sryo/Spoons/blob/main/AutoDMG.lua)**. For the people who treat a DMG download as a chore: drop it in Downloads, AutoDMG mounts, installs, and cleans up.

**[Palette](https://github.com/sryo/Spoons/tree/main/Palette)**. For the times muscle memory fails. Type a few letters and find any menu item, file, or app.
